About Me

Little Information About Me

My name is Brianjyot Singh. I was born in India and moved to America when I was around nine, I have lived in Washington since. I am currently a student at a college near my home. I find myself to be introverted, I like being alone, and as such my life is not that interesting. I like the colors of the sky, how they shift from blue to purple, gold to black. My first introduction to coding was a high school elective that I took in my senior year. At that time I had no plans of joining a career in this field, but recently I have found coding to be a lot of fun. I hope to grow with technology and, ultimately, create something that is uniquely mine.

As you can see, I had little success in art. Eventually, I found coding again.
